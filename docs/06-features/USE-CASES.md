# USE-CASES.md

**Enterprise AI Support V15 - Real-World Use Cases**

This document provides government-appropriate use case examples demonstrating how each persona benefits from the AI-Enhanced Customer Support platform.

---

## Overview

The platform serves 4 distinct personas with tailored capabilities:

1. **C-Level Executive** - Strategic decision making and board reporting
2. **CS Manager** - Team performance optimization and SLA management
3. **Support Agent** - Productivity boost and knowledge base assistance
4. **Client Success Manager** - Proactive client success and retention

---

## C-Level Executive Use Cases

### Use Case 1: Strategic Decision Making

**Persona**: C-Level Executive (CEO/CFO/CTO)

**Scenario**: CEO needs real-time customer health metrics for quarterly board meeting presentation

**Challenge**: Historical data scattered across multiple systems. No unified view of customer satisfaction trends and churn risk.

**Solution**: AI-powered executive dashboard aggregates data from all touchpoints, providing predictive analytics and trend analysis with natural language insights.

**Result**: Presented accurate churn risk analysis to the board, identified at-risk accounts worth $2M in ARR, and implemented proactive retention strategy.

**Metrics**:
- Time Saved: 4 hours (85% reduction from manual data gathering)
- Revenue Protected: $2M ARR (prevented churn)
- Data Accuracy: 99.8% (real-time sync across systems)

**Quick Actions**:
- "Show me SLA performance dashboard for this quarter"
- "Which customers are at highest risk of churning?"
- "Generate comprehensive executive dashboard summary"
- "Prepare metrics for board meeting presentation"

---

### Use Case 2: Board Metrics Preparation

**Persona**: C-Level Executive

**Metrics**:

## CS Manager Use Cases

### Use Case 1: Team Performance Optimization

**Persona**: CS Manager (Customer Support Operations Manager)

**Scenario**: CS Manager faces peak ticket volume during product launch with uneven agent workload distribution

**Challenge**: Some agents overwhelmed with 25+ tickets while others have <10. Manual reassignment takes too long, SLA breaches imminent.

**Solution**: AI analyzes real-time ticket complexity, agent skills, current workload, and response times. Recommends optimal ticket reassignments with one-click execution.

**Result**: Balanced workload across team in 5 minutes. Reduced average resolution time by 35%, prevented 47 SLA breaches, increased team satisfaction score from 6.2 to 8.9.

**Metrics**:
- Resolution Time: 35% faster (8.5 → 5.5 hours avg)
- SLA Compliance: 100% (47 breaches prevented)
- Team Satisfaction: 8.9/10 (+44% from 6.2)

**Quick Actions**:
- "Show me team workload dashboard"
- "Compare agent performance for this week"
- "Who is my most slacking agent?"
- "Who is my top performing agent?"
- "Recommend ticket reassignments for workload balance"

---

### Use Case 2: SLA Breach Prevention

**Persona**: CS Manager

**Scenario**: Manager needs to prevent SLA breaches during high-volume periods

**Challenge**: Manually tracking 100+ tickets approaching SLA deadlines is error-prone and time-consuming

**Solution**: AI monitors all tickets in real-time, predicts SLA breach risks, and alerts manager with recommended actions

**Result**: SLA compliance improved from 88% to 98%, customer escalations reduced by 60%

**Metrics**:
- SLA Compliance: 98% (up from 88%)
- Escalations: -60% reduction
- Manager Stress: -40% (automated monitoring)

## Support Agent Use Cases

### Use Case 1: Agent Productivity Boost

**Persona**: Support Agent (Senior Support Engineer)

**Scenario**: Support agent receives complex technical issue from enterprise client with 2-hour SLA deadline

**Challenge**: Unfamiliar with this specific integration issue. Searching internal docs, Slack, and past tickets could take 30+ minutes. Clock is ticking.

**Solution**: AI instantly surfaces 3 similar resolved tickets, relevant KB articles, and suggests response template with technical solution. Agent can customize and send in minutes.

**Result**: Resolved critical issue in 12 minutes (3x faster than 36-minute average). Customer praised quick response. Agent confidence increased, stress reduced.

**Metrics**:
- Resolution Time: 12 minutes (3x faster than avg)
- CSAT Score: 5/5 stars (customer praised speed)
- Agent Confidence: +60% (self-reported improvement)

**Quick Actions**:
- "Show me all my currently open support tickets"
- "How many tickets did AI resolve for me today?"
- "Search knowledge base for password reset"
- "Draft response for angry customer in ticket DESK-1002"
- "Prep notes for my 2 PM customer call"

---

### Use Case 2: AI-Assisted Resolution

**Persona**: Support Agent

**Scenario**: Agent receives 15 tickets in morning queue, 8 of which are common issues

**Challenge**: Manually responding to routine questions takes 3-4 hours, leaving no time for complex issues

**Solution**: AI auto-resolves 6 common tickets (password resets, account questions) with personalized responses, flags 2 for agent review

**Result**: Agent freed up 2.5 hours to focus on complex technical issues, overall resolution rate improved by 40%

**Metrics**:
- Time Saved: 2.5 hours per day
- Auto-Resolution Rate: 75% for routine tickets
- Agent Satisfaction: High (focus on interesting work)

## Client Success Manager Use Cases

### Use Case 1: Proactive Client Success

**Persona**: Client Success Manager (CSM)

**Scenario**: CSM manages 50 enterprise accounts and needs to identify accounts at risk of churn during renewal season

**Challenge**: No time to manually review product adoption, support ticket trends, and engagement metrics for all 50 accounts. Risk of missing early warning signs.

**Solution**: AI continuously monitors product usage, NPS trends, support ticket sentiment, and executive engagement. Surfaces top 8 at-risk accounts with recommended intervention actions.

**Result**: Proactively reached out to 8 at-risk accounts with personalized retention offers. Saved 6 accounts worth $480K ARR. Increased renewal rate from 82% to 94%.

**Metrics**:
- Accounts Saved: 6 of 8 (75% success rate)
- Revenue Retained: $480K ARR (prevented churn)
- Renewal Rate: 94% (+12% from 82%)

**Quick Actions**:
- "Show me health scores for my assigned clients"
- "Which clients have declining product adoption?"
- "Show upcoming renewals in next 90 days"
- "Identify upsell and cross-sell opportunities"
- "Schedule quarterly business reviews for top accounts"

---

### Use Case 2: Renewal Pipeline Management

**Persona**: Client Success Manager

**Scenario**: CSM needs to prioritize renewal conversations for 20 accounts renewing in Q1

**Challenge**: Manual analysis of contract values, health scores, and engagement data takes days

**Solution**: AI automatically ranks renewal accounts by risk level, contract value, and expansion potential with recommended actions

**Result**: Prioritized outreach schedule created in 15 minutes, secured 18 of 20 renewals with 30% expansion revenue

**Metrics**:
- Planning Time: 15 minutes (vs 2 days manual)
- Renewal Rate: 90% (18 of 20)
- Expansion Revenue: 30% upsell on renewals
